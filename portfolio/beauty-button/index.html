Beauty Button and Beauty Button Merchant

Code by Nadeem Archer, 26/08/2020

These two interconnected projects were built for Beauty Button, one as a B2C e-commerce site and the other as a B2B merchant portal.

Developed over 2 months using pure HTML, CSS and reusable component architecture, I created over 150 UI components for these sites. The key focuses were an immersive user experience for beauty product discovery and purchase, and providing merchants a comprehensive interface to manage their inventory and orders.

Beauty Button allowed users to easily browse and purchase from a catalog of curated beauty products. The merchant portal gave suppliers the tools to upload new products, track inventory, process orders, and view reports.

Although these sites are no longer live due to the owner's change in business plans, they represent one of my first end-to-end builds of a substantial e-commerce platform and merchant ecosystem. It gave me valuable full-stack development experience even early in my career.

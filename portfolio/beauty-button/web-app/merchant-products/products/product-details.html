Description

Not content with keeping our lips, lashes, lids and cheeks looking flawless, Huda Beauty has unveiled the secret to perfect skin: #FauxFilter Foundation. Offering a seamless, full-coverage matte finish that outlasts any others we’ve tried, this (frankly fabulous) formula will last through even the wildest of weather, workouts or emotional outbursts (though we hope it won’t have to). Water-resistant and practically budge-proof, this opaque base is blessed with a beautiful, petal-esque perfume and rich in skin-nourishing argan oil to respect your complexion as it instantly perfects. Packed with skin-unifying pigments to impart a gorgeous, demi-matte (but never flat) effect, a little of this goes a looooong way, so start with the tiniest bead and build up to achieve an HD-ready finish. The nemesis of blemishes, this effortlessly covers up a multitude of s(k)ins, from breakouts to scarring, dark spots and blackheads (it even disguises tattoos). Brilliant for bruises (it's ideal if you're braving bare legs), this guarantees a seamless finish that stays flawless.
